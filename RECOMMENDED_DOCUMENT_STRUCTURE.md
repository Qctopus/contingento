## WHY THIS STRUCTURE WORKS

### 1. **Risk-Centric = Natural Mental Model**
Small business owners think: "What's my biggest threat?" not "What are all the strategies?"

### 2. **Everything About One Risk in One Place**
Don't make them flip between "Section 3: Strategies" and "Section 4: Actions" to understand how to protect against hurricanes.

### 3. **Clear Priority**
EXTREME risks first â†’ HIGH risks â†’ MEDIUM risks â†’ LOW risks
They tackle biggest threats first with their limited budget.

### 4. **Integrated Decision-Making**
For each risk, they see:
- The threat
- Why it matters to THEM specifically
- ALL the options (budget to premium)
- Exact costs for each option
- How to actually do it
- Total investment needed

They can make ONE decision: "For hurricanes, I'll do Option B (plywood) for now, upgrade to Option A (shutters) next year."

### 5. **Total Cost Per Risk**
At the end of each risk section, they see: "To fully protect against hurricanes: JMD 58,000 minimum, JMD 145,000 recommended"

This lets them budget properly.
